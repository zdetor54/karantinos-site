Management

Konstantinos Karantinos graduated from Athens College in 1957 and acquired his Civil Engineering Diploma from NTUA in 1962. Since then he has been working as a Civil Engineer having over 45 years of experience in the field. He is a Public Works Contractor and in 1988 obtained the 4th degree Public Works Contractor licence for all five categories of projects, (i.e. building, highway, hydraulic, marine, and industrial projects). Konstantinos Karantinos is the Founder and Chief Executive of all the companies of the Group and has a prooven record of succesfull projects and buisness initiatives through the years.

Nicholas Karantinos Civil Engineer (MSc, DIC, BEng) graduated from Athens College in 1999 and completed his studies in UCL and Imperial College London. He joined the group in 2003 and is the Projects Director and Vice President of KARANTINOS CONSTRUCTION S.A. Nicholas Karantinos has an additional 5 year experience in construction working with Balfour Beatty Civil Engineering Ltd. and J&P S.A. in large scale building, and infrastructrure projects in UK and Athens.

Alexandros Karantinos Civil Engineer (BEng) graduated Athens College in 2000 and joined Imperial College London to acquire his Civil Engineering degree in 2007 from Kingston University. He worked for 3 years as a site engineer in Attiko Metro S.A. and J&P S.A. where he gained valuable experience in the field before joining KARANTINOS CONSTRUCTION S.A. After working in different positions within the Group Alexandros Karantinos has taken over management responsibilities in the Property and Development Division as well as in specific construction projects.
