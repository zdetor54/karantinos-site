Asset Management & Maintenance

Beyond construction and design services, our company also provides management and maintenance services to different types of assets from large scale industrial buildings to commercial real estate and hotel facilities. The asset management and maintenance division combines engineers, programmers, subcontractors and other specialists to offer whole life asset management services in cooperation with the client and tenant. As part of the management of building and infrastructure assets, the maintenance requirements are identified and carried out always.
