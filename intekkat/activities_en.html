Activities in the field of environmental protection

INTEKKAT S.A., is especially interested in issues concerning the environment and has had the project management projects in the following areas:

  • Drainage - Organic Cleaning - Waste Management.
    Includes interventions in these highly sensitive areas of residential centres, to improve the living conditions of residents and preserve their wellbeing. (Peloponnese region - industrial and tourist facilities).
  • Regeneration of Urban Cities and Beaches.
    Includes interventions in suburban city centers to improve the quality of life for residents:
    • Studies of the general development plan aiming at the removal of distorting characteristics and environmental burdens in cities with over 15,000 inhabitants
    • Studies and projects beautify public spaces - monuments, beaches configurations and restoration of historic buildings.
  • Environmental Protection
    Manufacture of Biological Cleaning Facilities, Organization - Management - Function of waste repositories, improved waste management systems, research and study for the establishment of buffer zones of mineral and thermal water.
  • Forestry Projects.
    Work in this area is aimed at protecting the natural environment, offering recreational opportunities to the people and educating on environmental issues. These activities are:
    • Reforestation of woodlandsν
    • Improvement of degraded forests
    • Fire protection
    • Arrange of torrents
    • Construction of forest roads
    • National parks, aesthetic forests, hunting
  • Protection of consumers of agricultural products from non-permissible pesticide residues.
    Includes building and laboratory equipment of TUC Nafplio for systematic monitoring of pesticide residues in commodities to:
    • The protection of consumer health
    • The rational use of pesticides
    • Reducing the consequences of misuse of pesticides in humans and in different ecosystems
    • Reducing the cost of production of agricultural products
  • Agricultural Program alerts to:
    • The rational control of pests and diseases of major crops in the region
    • The reduction of chemical interventions
    • The reduction of toxic substances from pesticides that affect the population as well as the environment
    • The reduction of the cost of production of agricultural products
  • Relocation of livestock facilities in order to protect public health
  • Handling and processing of waste holdings from livestock facilities
  • Studies for the development of aquaculture units
