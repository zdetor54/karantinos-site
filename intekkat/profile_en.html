The consulting and design services of the group are provided by INTEKKAT S.A. and its sector K.Karantinos & Associates (Engineering & Design) respectively.

INTEKKAT S.A. ENGINEERS - CONSULTANTS is providing management and engineering consulting services. The company’s role is to advice leading companies and public organizations on issues of strategy, organization, technology, and operations achieving significant, tangible and lasting improvements in performance. Since the founding of INTEKKAT S.A., our target has been to deliver results through fast but comprehensive analysis and rapid implementation.

The consulting services of INTEKKAT S.A. are summarized with the following sectors:

  • FEASIBILITY AND DEVELOPMENT STUDIES
  • SPECIAL ENVIRONMENTAL- ECOLOGICAL STUDIES
  • DESIGN
  • PROJECT AND PROGRAM MANAGEMENT
  • TRAINING
  • MARKETING
  • RENEWABLE ENERGY SOURCES

The engineering origin of INTEKKAT S.A. allows us to successfully combine management and engineering solutions for our clients minimizing the overall project and operational costs and simultaneously increasing efficiency.

K.Karantinos & Associates is the engineering and design sector of INTEKKAT S.A. that has established a reputation for architecture, civil, electro-mechanicalengineering and interior design based upon a commitment to design excellence, technological innovation and environmental awareness. Our current portfolio demonstrates buildings serving industrial, residential and commercial purposes as well as cultural and social needs.

With many years experience of working with major clients on a wide range of projects, K.Karantinos & Associates has the capacity and expertise to drive large projects through from conceptual design to the handover of the completed buildings. K.Karantinos & Associates has designed industrial and office buildings, residential and commercial complexes, villas, hospitals, hotels and touristic resorts.
